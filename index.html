Welcome to my gallery.


I am from Poland but I live in England. I'm a guy who believes in destiny and my life might have sounded joyful at first but quickly turned into pain.. I wanted to be normal just like anyone else. I want a relationship, then family, happy life and final death but it has become something different, a completely different story. I became an artist because I listened to my heart and let my pain decide for me... To me it is something special and unique because I feel like my artwork does improve every time from my emotions.
