Who I Am

My name is Tom Pocelujko (nickname Tomartguy). I was born in Poland but I am based around Dorset.
I became an artist as Tomartguy because I listened to my heart and let my pain decide for me... To me, it is something special and unique because I feel like my artwork does improve every time from my emotions. I never went to any courses in art I believe I have unique own imagination and am smart enough to study by myself, sometimes art is about what you have to offer to the world.

A Few Words About Me

I had been in a good long-term relationship.

We loved each other but something changed and it turned into dust. This is when I became Tom Art Guy because I love art.

Finding something that I enjoy did help and save me from the lowest life failure or a broken dream.

Building my character and attributes is everything.

I enjoy making artwork and when I create a masterpiece with good quality I feel like I won the lottery because I created something new for the World. I contributed.
